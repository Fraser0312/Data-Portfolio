# Summary Tab

This page provides a side-by-side comparison of a customer’s current position against an amended scenario, highlighting the financial impact of proposed changes. It summarises key metrics such as operating result, sales, margin, and cost-to-serve, with variance analysis to clearly show the impact.Users can adjust inputs such as delivery method and cost assumptions to model different scenarios, with both current and annualised views to assess short- and long-term effects. The accompanying visual reinforces changes in cost structure and profitability, supporting more informed decisions around pricing, service levels, and customer strategy.

# Current Position Tab 

This page provides a detailed view of the customer’s current financial and operational position, combining key commercial KPIs with a breakdown of operating and accounting costs. It highlights overall margin performance alongside contribution metrics, giving a clear view of both total and incremental profitability.The report also models the impact of alternative delivery frequencies (twice weekly, weekly, and monthly) on cost-to-serve and net profit, enabling users to assess operational efficiency and identify opportunities to improve margins through changes in service levels.

# Amendable Business Tab

This page presents a comprehensive view of a customer’s current performance by combining key commercial metrics, cost breakdowns, and profitability analysis. It highlights sales, margin, order behaviour, and delivery activity alongside a detailed breakdown of operating costs to show how cost-to-serve impacts overall profitability.It also compares full margin against contribution performance, allowing users to understand both total profitability and the customer’s incremental value once fixed costs are excluded.In addition, the page models the financial impact of different delivery frequency scenarios (twice weekly, weekly, and monthly), showing how changes in service levels affect delivery costs, net profit, and margin. This enables users to identify more efficient delivery strategies and optimise profitability at a customer level.
