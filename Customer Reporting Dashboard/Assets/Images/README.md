# Summary Tab

This page provides an overview of customer performance, combining key KPIs with trend and comparative analysis. It highlights year-on-year performance across sales, gross profit, and order volumes, giving a quick view of growth and overall account health. Users can filter by sales type and account to analyse performance across different revenue streams. The monthly trend chart compares current versus prior year sales and margin, enabling identification of seasonality and performance shifts. Additional insights are provided through the product mix breakdown, showing the proportion of core versus non-core sales, helping users understand revenue composition and identify opportunities to optimise product strategy.

# Section Breakdown Tab 

This page provides a detailed breakdown of performance by product section, highlighting both growth opportunities and areas of decline. It identifies the top-performing and underperforming categories based on sales movement, enabling users to quickly focus on key drivers of change.The sales vs growth scatter plot further enhances this by positioning each section based on revenue and growth rate, helping to distinguish between high-value growth areas, stable performers, and declining segments. The treemap complements this view by showing the overall sales contribution of each section, giving a clear understanding of product mix and relative importance within the account.Together, these visuals support targeted decision-making by helping users prioritise growth areas, address declining categories, and optimise product strategy at a granular level.

# Product Detail Tab 

This page provides a detailed product-level view of customer performance, combining key KPIs with granular sales data. It highlights year-on-year trends across sales, orders, and gross profit, enabling users to quickly assess overall performance at a filtered level (by section, family, or supplier). The top-selling products chart identifies the highest revenue-generating items, while the detailed table provides a breakdown of sales, quantities, and year-on-year comparisons at an individual product level. Together, these insights allow users to identify top-performing products, track changes in demand, and support more informed decisions around product focus, pricing, and range optimisation.
